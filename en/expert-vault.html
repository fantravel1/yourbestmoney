Masterclasses, Interviews & Deep Dives

Twelve expert sessions spanning every critical area of personal finance. Each one is free, on-demand, and designed to leave you with actionable takeaways.

Building a Bulletproof Portfolio from Scratch

Dr. Elena Ramirez, CFP & Wealth Advisor

Learn how to select asset classes, determine allocation ratios based on your risk tolerance, and set up automatic rebalancing. This masterclass covers everything from your first $100 to managing a six-figure portfolio — with real-world examples and common mistakes to avoid.

⏱ 45 min 🎓 Beginner Masterclass

The Psychology Behind Market Panics

Prof. Marcus Chen, Ph.D. Behavioral Economics

Why do smart investors sell at the worst possible time? Explore the neuroscience of fear in financial markets, understand herd behavior, and learn the cognitive biases — loss aversion, anchoring, recency bias — that cost people thousands. Walk away with a personal panic-proof action plan.

⏱ 38 min 🎓 Intermediate Deep Dive

Real Estate Investing Without Millions

Angela Torres, Licensed Realtor & Property Investor

You do not need to be wealthy to invest in real estate. This interview covers REITs, real estate crowdfunding, house hacking, and the math behind rental property cash flow. Angela shares the exact strategy she used to go from renter to owning four investment properties in five years.

⏱ 42 min 🎓 Beginner Interview
4

Crypto Wallet Security & Scam Prevention

David Okafor, Blockchain Security Researcher

Billions are lost to crypto scams every year. This masterclass teaches you how to identify phishing attacks, secure your wallets (hot vs. cold), recognize rug pulls, verify smart contracts, and protect your seed phrases. Includes a step-by-step security checklist you can use immediately.

⏱ 50 min 🎓 All Levels Masterclass
5

Tax-Advantaged Wealth Building Strategies

Sarah Kim, CPA & Tax Strategist

The tax code is full of legal advantages most people never use. Learn how to maximize 401(k) contributions, understand Roth conversions, harvest tax losses, use HSAs as stealth retirement accounts, and structure your investments to minimize your lifetime tax burden — all explained without jargon.

⏱ 40 min 🎓 Intermediate Deep Dive
6

Retirement Planning at Every Age

Robert Nakamura, CFP & Retirement Specialist

Whether you are 22 or 55, this session meets you where you are. Robert breaks down how much you really need, the power of starting early versus catching up later, Social Security optimization, withdrawal strategies, and the "4% rule" — including when it does and does not apply.

⏱ 48 min 🎓 All Levels Masterclass
7

Estate Planning for Ordinary Families

Victoria Osei, Estate Planning Attorney

Estate planning is not just for the rich. Learn why everyone needs a will, the difference between revocable and irrevocable trusts, how to set up beneficiary designations correctly, power of attorney basics, and how to have the money conversation with your family without the awkwardness.

⏱ 35 min 🎓 Beginner Interview
8

The Debt-Free Blueprint

James Walker, Financial Coach & Author

Debt is not a moral failure — it is a math problem with emotional fuel. James paid off $127,000 in student loans and credit card debt in four years. He shares his exact system: avalanche vs. snowball methods, negotiating lower interest rates, balance transfers, and the psychology of staying motivated when progress feels slow.

⏱ 36 min 🎓 Beginner Masterclass
9

Behavioral Finance: Why Smart People Make Bad Money Decisions

Dr. Amara Johnson, Ph.D. Financial Psychology

Intelligence does not protect you from money mistakes. This deep dive explores the Dunning-Kruger effect in investing, emotional spending triggers, the sunk cost fallacy, mental accounting traps, and how your upbringing shapes your financial behavior. Includes practical exercises to rewire your money mindset.

⏱ 44 min 🎓 Intermediate Deep Dive
10

The Index Fund Revolution

Patricia Duval, CFA & Portfolio Manager

Why do index funds outperform 90% of actively managed funds over 20 years? Patricia explains the data, walks through how to choose between S&P 500, total market, and international index funds, compares expense ratios, and shares the "three-fund portfolio" that many financial planners recommend for lifetime wealth building.

⏱ 32 min 🎓 Beginner Masterclass
11

Alternative Assets: Beyond Stocks and Bonds

Michael Ashworth, Alternative Investment Analyst

Commodities, precious metals, art, wine, farmland, private credit, collectibles — the world of alternative assets is vast. Michael explains which alternatives actually make sense for everyday investors, the liquidity trade-offs, minimum investment thresholds, and how to allocate 5-15% of your portfolio to non-correlated assets.

⏱ 41 min 🎓 Intermediate Deep Dive
12

Budgeting Mastery: The System That Actually Sticks

Lisa Chang, Financial Educator & Budgeting Coach

Most budgets fail within 30 days because they rely on willpower instead of systems. Lisa teaches the "Pay Yourself First" method, zero-based budgeting, envelope systems (digital and physical), automation strategies, and how to budget for irregular income. Includes downloadable templates and a 30-day challenge.

⏱ 30 min 🎓 Beginner Interview
